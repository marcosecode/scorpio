How To Join ?

Download Discord

To download Discord, start by visiting the official website at discord.com. If you’re on a PC, click the "Download for Windows" button to get the installer, then follow the prompts to complete the installation. For mobile users, download the app from the App Store or Google Play. After installing, open Discord and create a new account or log in with your existing credentials. Once logged in, you can join servers, chat with friends, and customize your profile to stay connected across all your devices.

Pass Whitelist

To pass the whitelist for Scorpio Roleplay, you must first join the official Discord server. Then, head to the server’s website and fill out the whitelist application form, providing details about your in-game character such as name, age, and background story. After submitting the form, you will be required to complete a voice interview on Discord, where the staff will assess your understanding of the rules and your seriousness about roleplay. Once you pass the interview, you will receive the Whitelisted role and be ready to join the server and start your journey.

Download Samp

To join the Scorpio Roleplay server after passing the whitelist, you need to have GTA San Andreas installed on your PC or mobile device. On PC, download and install the SA-MP (San Andreas Multiplayer) program from the official site, then open it, add the Scorpio server’s IP address under "Favorites," and join using your character’s name. On mobile, use a multiplayer-compatible GTA San Andreas app, add the server IP you received after whitelist approval, and log in with your character name. The whitelist ensures a controlled and immersive roleplaying environment for all players.
