❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How can I apply for the whitelist?

Simply visit our whitelist page and fill out the application form with accurate and honest information. Make sure to answer all required questions clearly.

2. How long does it take to get approved?

Whitelist applications are reviewed within 24 to 48 hours. In peak times, it may take slightly longer, so please be patient.

3. Do I need previous roleplay experience?

No prior experience is required. However, having basic knowledge of roleplay rules and behavior will improve your chances of getting approved.

4. What are the server rules?

All server members must follow our roleplay rules, which include respecting other players, avoiding powergaming, metagaming, and staying in character. Full rules are available on our Discord or website.

5. How can I support the server?

You can support us by boosting our Discord server, donating, or simply being an active and positive part of the community. Boosters will receive special recognition in our Supporters section.

6. What age do I need to be to join?

You must be at least 16 years old to join our server and apply for the whitelist.

7. What happens if my application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, don’t worry. You’ll receive feedback, and you’re welcome to reapply after making the necessary improvements.

8. How do I contact the staff for help?

You can reach our team through our Discord support channel. We’re always available to help with issues, questions, or concerns.

Why Scorpio ?
